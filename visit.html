Getting There
Flying: To get to wedding activities is approximately a 90 minute drive from Sea-Tac Airport or a 3 hour drive from Portland International Airport
From Seattle: There are multiple ferries that service Kitsap Penninsula. Note that the estimated drive time above does not include ferry wait and loading times (if applicable). A standard vehicle + 2 passangers will cost approximately $22 Fare info.

  • Seattle::Bainbridge Island (60 minut crossing, schedule)
  • Seattle::Bremerton (60 min crossing, schedule)

  • Alternatively, drive across at the Tacoma Narrows bridge.
